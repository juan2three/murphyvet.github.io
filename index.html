Welcome

Murphy Veterinary Clinic is committed to providing each patient with an exceptional level of care and attention. We are proud to be physician owned and operated. Our dedicated staff works together as a team to provide high-quality comprehensive care. Our compassionate and experienced doctors take pride in taking each patient's individual needs into consideration.

Why Murphyvet?

Murphy Veterinary Clinic with Dr. Susan Wayne has been serving families from Sanford, Lake Mary, Orlando and the surrounding areas for over 25 Years. We are committed to providing each patient with an exceptional level of care and personalized attention. Our goal is to be your partner in providing the compassionate and quality care you deserve for your companions (dogs and cats). Our dedicated staff is here to help you with medical care, surgery, boarding, baths, and much more. We understand that they're more than just a pet. We will treat you and your companions like family. In addition to traditional medicine we offer acupuncture, herbal supplements, and assist with special dietary needs.
